ABOUT SAE MMMUT

The Society of Automotive Engineers (SAE) Collegiate Club at Madan Mohan Malaviya University of Technology (MMMUT) is a student-run organization dedicated to fostering innovation and excellence in automotive engineering.

Our team brings together students from diverse engineering disciplines to collaborate on cutting-edge automotive projects, participate in national and international competitions, and develop practical skills beyond the classroom.

Since our inception, we've won multiple accolades at prestigious events like SAE BAJA, SUPRA, and Aeromodelling competitions, establishing MMMUT as a formidable presence in the automotive engineering arena.
